Font Visibility & Contrast Guidelines

Proper font weight and color choices ensure readability across light and dark themes.

Light Backgrounds (White, Light Gray)

‚úÖ Recommended
  • Headlines: Navy (#0B1020) at 700-800 weight
  • Body Text: Gray Dark (#1E1E1E) at 400 weight
  • Secondary Text: Gray (#8C8C8C) at 400-500 weight
  • Contrast Ratios: Navy on White = 14.5:1, Gray Dark on White = 12.1:1
‚ùå Avoid
  • Light gray text (poor contrast)
  • Font weights below 400 for body text
  • Orange text on white (3.2:1 ‚Äî fails WCAG AA)
  • Font sizes below 14px for body content

Dark Backgrounds (Navy, Navy Light)

‚úÖ Recommended
  • Headlines: Warm White (#F5EDE6) at 700-800 weight
  • Body Text: Warm White (#F5EDE6) at 400 weight
  • Secondary Text: Gray Light (#CFCFCF) at 400-500 weight
  • Accents: Orange (#FF6B1A) at 600-700 weight
  • Contrast Ratios: Warm White on Navy = 12.8:1, Orange on Navy = 7.2:1
‚ùå Avoid
  • Pure white (#FFFFFF) ‚Äî too harsh, causes eye strain
  • Font weights below 400 (disappears on dark)
  • Gray (#8C8C8C) on Navy (poor contrast)
  • Thin strokes or light weights for small text

Weight Selection by Context

Element Light Theme Dark Theme Reason
H1/H2 Headlines 800 weight 800 weight Maximum impact and visibility
H3/H4 Subheads 700 weight 700 weight Clear hierarchy without overwhelming
Body Text 400 weight 400-500 weight Dark bg needs slightly heavier for clarity
UI Labels 500 weight 500-600 weight Needs to stand out from body text
CTAs/Buttons 600 weight 600-700 weight Must be immediately actionable
üí° Key Principle: On dark backgrounds, bump font weights up by 100 (e.g., 400‚Üí500) for equivalent perceived weight. Dark backgrounds make text appear lighter due to halation effect.

Imagery & Style

Photography Style

Iconography

Icon Style

  • Stroke-based icons (not filled) for UI
  • 2px stroke weight at default size
  • Rounded corners (2-4px radius)
  • 24x24px base grid

Color Usage

  • Primary: Warm White (#F5EDE6) on dark backgrounds
  • Accent: Orange (#FF6B1A) for active/selected states
  • Disabled: Gray (#8C8C8C)
